# Soda-Pow!
In a world where every morning begins with a choice of drink, the Great Taste War has erupted. Four factions clash — lemonade lovers, coffee and tea enthusiasts, energy drink fanatics, and wine connoisseurs. Each one is determined to prove that their flavor sets the rhythm of life.
But you’re a free spirit. No one can force you to drink what you don’t like! You’ll have to make tough choices, fight for your freedom, and fend off relentless flavor preachers.
Fight back! Discover new lemonade combinations! Which side will you join — and what will you sacrifice for the taste of victory?
